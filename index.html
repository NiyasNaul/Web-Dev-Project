How to Use Dice Roller?


What you need to do is, first enter how many dice you need then write d and then write your dice number. Meaning that how many sided dice you want. For example when I write 2d10, the dice roller will roll 2 dice with 10 sides and each dice will give me numbers between 1-10.


Is It Realy Random?

You can check that by clicking calculate randomness.It will give you 1000000d10 dice rolls result, which number how many times rolled.
